Who We Are?

One of the most prestigious club of BUP

The BUP Robotics Club at Bangladesh University of Professionals (BUP) is a student-led organization dedicated to nurturing talent in robotics and innovative research. Founded by ICT undergraduates, the club engages students in cutting-edge projects and activities to develop their skills and foster a culture of creativity in science and technology. Through workshops, seminars and competitions, the BUP Robotics Club provides a platform for students to explore and excel in the field of robotics, shaping the next generation of technological leaders
